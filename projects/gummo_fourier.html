    • Fourier Analysis of "Gummo" by 6ix9ine

    This project computes the Fourier series of the first four bars of the song Gummo by 6ix9ine. The goal is to approximate the waveform using the first 1000 terms of the Fourier series and analyze its harmonic structure.

    • Computed Fourier coefficients and reconstructed the waveform.

    • Used Python with NumPy, SciPy, and Matplotlib for analysis and visualization.

    • Included a LaTeX-rendered equation of the full Fourier series.
